Meteor Grove Software was founded in 2012 by Amanda Wixted. We are an independent iOS development & consulting shop located in Austin, Texas, USA.
Amanda is a seasoned veteran of the mobile game industry. The first engineer hired on Zynga's mobile team, she lead the iOS development of FarmVille, Mafia Wars, and Zynga Poker.
Previously, at Namco Networks, she lead the development of Pac-Man and Ms Pac-Man to coincide with the launch of the App Store for iPhone. Before that, she worked on a multitude of games for J2ME handsets.
We are excited to help you bring your ideas to iPhone and iPad. To discuss your project, email amanda@amandawixted.com.
